# FAQ - general

### What is Installment 0-0-4?

This is an installment plan with the participation of the BNPL Partner service. A payment method that provides the ability to:

- Purchase an item on installment for 4 months
- No commissions or overpayments
- Instant approval

### How do I make a purchase on installment?

- Add items to your cart;
- Choose "Installment 0-0-4" as the payment method;
- Specify your phone number, IIN (the phone number must be registered in the mobile citizens database - eGov mobile);
- How to register a number in the mobile citizens database (eGov mobile):

Follow the link: [https://egov.kz/cms/ru/articles/mobilecitizen](https://egov.kz/cms/ru/articles/mobilecitizen)

Follow the on-screen instructions – the process will take up to 2 minutes.

### What are the conditions for purchasing an item on installment 0-0-4?

- Age from 18 to 60 years;
- Order amount from 6,000 to 200,000 tenge;
- Resident of the Republic of Kazakhstan;
- No overdue debt;
- No tax debt.

### Why is it profitable to buy an item on installment 0-0-4?

- No commissions;
- No overpayments;
- Installment for 4 months;

### Why couldn't I buy an item on installment 0-0-4?

- In each specific case, the decision is made individually and depends on the quality of the credit history.
- Try again after 14 days, during which time the credit history is updated.

### How do I pay for an item purchased on Installment 0-0-4?

- Download/open the **Partner Bank** app of the bnpl.kz service, **"Bank CenterCredit" – bcc.kz**;
- Log in or register;
- From the app's home page go to the section:

Services → Mall → My orders → Authorization → Select order → Pay now

### Is there a commission or overpayment for a purchase on installment 0-0-4?

No. You can buy without commissions or overpayments if you pay on time.

### What is the penalty amount if I am late with a monthly payment?

For late payment of a monthly payment, you will be charged a fixed penalty of 2200 tenge added to the payment amount.

### When do I pay for an item purchased on installment?

- When placing the order, a preliminary payment schedule will be displayed;
- We will remind you in advance of the date and amount of the monthly payment via SMS;
- The installment plan is activated only after receiving the item/service;
- All information about the order (schedule, amount due, early/partial early repayment) For this, in the **Partner Bank** **"Bank CenterCredit"** app, from the app's home page go to the section:

Services → Mall → My orders → Authorization → Select order

### For what amount can I purchase an item on installment 0-0-4?

You can choose items for an amount from 6,000 KZT to 200,000 KZT.

### Can I repay Installment 0-0-4 early?

Yes, you can repay the installment plan early without additional commissions. For this, in the **Partner Bank** **"Bank CenterCredit"** app, from the app's home page go to the section:

Services → Mall → My orders → Authorization → Select order → Pay now

### Can I pay the installment with a card from another bank?

Payments are accepted only from a **Partner Bank** **"Bank CenterCredit"** card in the bcc.kz app.

### How do I top up my Bank CenterCredit card?

- Topping up the card by transfer through other banks' apps using account details or card number (commissions should be checked with the sending bank);
- Topping up the card at **Partner Bank** **"Bank CenterCredit"** ATMs;
- Through a bank branch teller.
