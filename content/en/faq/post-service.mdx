# FAQ - post-service

### How do I pay off the installment plan early?

To pay off the installment plan early, you need to go to the client's personal cabinet at the link: [https://bnpl.kz/customer-cabinet](https://bnpl.kz/customer-cabinet). You can also access the personal cabinet through the main BNPL site at [https://bnpl.kz/](https://bnpl.kz/). To log in to the personal cabinet, you will need to enter the user's phone number.

### How can I know when and what amount will be charged from the card?

Information about the charging of subsequent payments can be found in the personal cabinet in the "My installments" section: [https://bnpl.kz/customer-cabinet](https://bnpl.kz/customer-cabinet) or via the main page at [https://bnpl.kz/](https://bnpl.kz/). To log in to the personal cabinet, you will need to enter the user's phone number.

### What happens if there isn't enough on the card for the monthly charge?

If there is not enough on the specified payment card for the installment amount required on the charge date, a one-time penalty of 2,200 tg is imposed on the client for each overdue payment. One overdue payment equals one month.

### Is a partial return or buyout of an order possible? If so, when will the installment amount change?

Yes, a partial return is possible. The partial return amount is subtracted from the original amount specified in the installment application.

### How is the money charged? Will there be any notification?

- We notify clients about the charging of funds from the specified card 2 days in advance. The charge is made automatically on the date specified in the payment schedule.
- If the money is not charged from the card by 12:00 on the payment date, you should contact customer support at [customer@bnpl.kz](mailto:customer@bnpl.kz).

### Is there a commission or overpayment for a purchase on installment 0-0-4?

No. You can buy without commissions or overpayments if you pay on time.

### What is the penalty amount if I am late with a monthly payment?

For late payment of a monthly payment, you will be charged a fixed penalty of 2,200 tenge added to the payment amount.

### If I have an active installment 0-0-4 for 200,000 tenge, can I take out another installment through BNPL?

The maximum BNPL limit is 200,000 tenge. After successfully repaying the first payment (and/or subsequent ones) in the amount of 50,000 tenge, you will again have the opportunity to apply for an installment plan for an amount equivalent to the paid-off debt on the previously approved, active installment plan.
