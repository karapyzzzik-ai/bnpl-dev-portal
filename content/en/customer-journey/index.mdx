The partner redirects the client to the received link (redirectLink)

### Widget (Modal window)

**Widget** (within the BNPL product concept) is a js library that allows setting up the product on the merchant's checkout page, without redirecting the client to our UI. A demo can be viewed here.

_\*We recommend using this method for seamless integration._
